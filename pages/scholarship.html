We reward metro-Atlanta junior and senior student-athletes who have faced loss, hardship, or season-ending injury — with scholarships paid directly to their institution. We can't wait to read your story.

What we fund

The REACH Scholarship is awarded annually to junior and senior student-athletes from our partner high schools. Funds are paid directly to the receiving institution and applied to tuition, books, fees, or other verified institutional costs.

We don't just write a check and walk away. Recipients are invited into our mentorship community — Saturday Sessions, networking events, and a standing relationship with our board and alumni.

Who we look for

We don't have a GPA cutoff. We look for scholars who have faced real challenges — the loss of a parent, financial instability, a season-ending injury, caregiving responsibilities — and have kept showing up anyway. If that's you, we want to hear from you.

Eligibility

The basics

Junior or senior

Currently enrolled at one of our 11 partner high schools.

Student-athlete

On a varsity, JV, or club team in good standing.

Documented hardship

Loss, injury, financial, or caregiving challenges.

Metro Atlanta

City of Atlanta, South Fulton, or Clayton County.
