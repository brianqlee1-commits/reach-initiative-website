Our Programs.
The Three Ms.

Three programs, one cycle. We invest directly, we teach what school doesn't, and we ask graduates to come back and do it again for the next class.

01
Monetary Support

Scholarships at graduation

We reward academic success with direct scholarships for junior and senior student-athletes — paid to the institution to ease post-graduation burdens. Tuition, books, fees, and other verified institutional costs at the moment they matter most.

Scholarship overview
02
Mentorship

Saturday Sessions, monthly

We match former student-athletes who've achieved professionally and personally with current scholars, building strong bonds and exposing mentees to life lessons absent from the traditional school curriculum — SMART goals, financial literacy, networking, dress for success, and recruiting.

Mentorship overview
03
Motivation

Mentees become mentors

We motivate past program participants to reach the next generation of student-athletes — creating a cycle of support within metro Atlanta. The bridge from one student-athlete to the next is built and walked by the same people, again and again.
