Our Story

Filling a gap
no one else was.

Too many talented student-athletes in metro Atlanta hit senior year carrying weight no scholarship application asks about — a parent lost, an injury that ended a recruiting season, a family that can't cover the gap between a partial offer and a full tuition bill. They show up to graduation with a diploma in one hand and uncertainty in the other.

REACH was founded to meet that exact need: direct scholarship dollars at the moment they matter, plus mentorship and a community that doesn't disappear after the check clears. We awarded our first scholarships in 2020 and 2021, paused through COVID, and are re-launching for the 2026–2027 school year — bigger, more focused, and built around the three Ms our scholars actually need: Monetary support, Mentorship, and Mental wellness.

Brian Q. Lee
Founder · Westlake '05 · Atlanta, GA
