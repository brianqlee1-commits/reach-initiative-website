How it works

Our Mentoring Program matches former student-athletes who have demonstrated professional and personal achievement with current scholars. The bond is real, the time commitment is monthly, and the goal is simple: expose mentees to life lessons that aren't on the syllabus.

Saturday Sessions are our flagship gathering — a single Saturday every month where mentors, mentees, alumni, and partners come together for a focused topic, real conversation, and a shared meal.
