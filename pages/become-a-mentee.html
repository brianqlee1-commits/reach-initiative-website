Become a
Mentee.

A mentor in your corner for the year. Saturday Sessions once a month. People who've been where you're going. We're glad you're here.

Who Can Apply

Mentees are…

  • Sophomore, junior, or senior at one of our partner schools
  • A student-athlete (varsity, JV, or club)
  • Open to one Saturday a month, Sept – May
  • Ready to show up and engage — that's the whole bar
